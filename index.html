What is Adalod?

AdaLod is a project that intends to focus on the study of the domain represented by the historical figure of Ada Lovelace and to explore the semantically connected items through the creation of an organized environment of linked open data that can represent knowledge related to the chosen entity. Dec. 10, 1815 - Nov. 27, 1852
The world's first computer programmer, Ada Lovelace, a British noblewoman and mathematician, was known for her invaluable contribution to Charles Babbage's analytical machine and especially for the creation of an algorithm capable of generating Bernoulli numbers. Through the latter, Ada proved that she understood that computational machines could process any kind of information thus representing the first programmer...
